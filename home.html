Project Overview


As humans continue to modernize and industrialize the earth, the impact is clearly shown throughout the world.
Global Warming and plastic pollution are no longer a joke and can be destructive to all life.
The Earth is no longer what it once was and it desperately needs our help.
This website was created in hopes to spread awareness and initiate people to fight the clear danger our planet earth and creatures are facing.
